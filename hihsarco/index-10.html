关于我们

        我们是一家流浪动物救助平台，目的是为了使更多流浪的小动物感受到爱和温暖。我们的宗旨是让每一只流浪动物都能得到救助，为更多流浪动物提供一个遮风挡雨的家。我们提倡通过领养来代替买卖，希望能够给更多流浪的动物找到一个爱他的主人和温暖的家。
        生存保证：认真对待每一只小动物，让他们在食物方面没有后顾之忧。
        健康保证：定期找到宠物医生对他们的身体进行检查，保障动物的健康情况。
        训练保证：对收容的动物进行基本的训练，如有特别急躁的会聘请专业宠物训练师对其进行训练。

        我们在收容到流浪的动物后会对每一只动物进行身体检查，保证动物们的健康。每天定时对宠物进行喂食，每年定期给动物检查身体，给动物们的生存情况提供保障。我们也希望大家都能爱护动物，每只动物都能够健健康康。愿世上没有流浪动物，让我们早日能够功成身退！

We are a stray animal rescue platform, aiming to provide love and warmth to more stray animals. Our goal is to rescue every stray animal and provide them with a shelter from the storm. We advocate for adoption over buying and hope to find loving owners and warm homes for more stray animals.
Our guarantees:
Survival Guarantee: We take care of each little animal, ensuring they never go hungry.
Health Guarantee: We commit to regular vet check-ups for their health.
Training Guarantee: We provide basic training for sheltered animals and hire professional trainers for those with special needs.
After taking in a stray, we conduct thorough health checks for each animal to ensure their well-being. Daily feeding and regular check-ups are part of our routine to safeguard their survival. We also hope everyone can cherish animals, so that every animal can live a healthy life. Here's to a world without stray animals and our eventual retirement!
